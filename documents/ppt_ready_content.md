## Slide 2 - Project Overview
**What is DriveCare?**
- DriveCare is a mobile-first vehicle management application
- It helps users manage everyday vehicle ownership tasks from one platform
- Combines vehicle records, maintenance tracking, safety tools, and eco insights
- Built with React Native frontend and Node.js + MongoDB backend

## Slide 3 - Problem Statement
**Why this project is needed**
- Vehicle owners often track records in different places
- Fuel expenses are hard to monitor over time
- Maintenance schedules are often forgotten
- Important documents can expire unnoticed
- Emergency communication during accidents is too slow
- Most users do not have a simple way to understand vehicle health

## Slide 4 - Project Objective
**Main Objective**
- To simplify vehicle ownership and maintenance
- To improve safety with emergency support features
- To help users reduce missed services and document expiry issues
- To increase fuel cost awareness and tracking accuracy
- To encourage better vehicle care and sustainable driving habits

## Slide 5 - Target Users
**Who can use DriveCare?**
- Individual car and bike owners
- Families managing multiple vehicles
- Daily commuters
- Students and working professionals with personal vehicles
- Users who want emergency support and quick-access safety tools
- Eco-conscious drivers who want to measure efficiency improvements

## Slide 6 - Technology Stack
**Technologies Used**
- Frontend: React Native with Expo
- Navigation: React Navigation
- Storage: AsyncStorage
- API Communication: Axios
- Backend: Node.js and Express.js
- Database: MongoDB with Mongoose
- Authentication: JWT-based login system
- Location Services: Expo Location
- Emergency SMS Support: Twilio integration

## Slide 7 - Core Features
**Main Features of the App**
- User registration and login

- Fuel tracking
- Service and maintenance logging
- Vehicle health dashboard
- Document tracking
- SOS emergency assistance
- Eco impact tracking
- Profile and emergency contacts management

## Slide 8 - Vehicle Management Module
**Vehicle Management**
- Add and manage multiple vehicles
- Store make, model, year, fuel type, odometer, and plate number
- Edit and delete vehicle information
- Set a primary vehicle for quick access
- Maintain separate logs for each vehicle

## Slide 9 - Fuel Tracking Module
**Fuel Tracking**
- Record each fuel refill
- Store fuel amount, cost, and odometer reading
- View fuel history for each vehicle
- Estimate total fuel spending over time
- Calculate average fuel efficiency based on logs

## Slide 10 - Service & Maintenance Module
**Service and Maintenance Tracking**
- Log service activities such as oil change or tire rotation
- Save maintenance notes and history
- Keep a service record for every vehicle
- Help users stay aware of maintenance needs
- Support better long-term vehicle condition management

## Slide 11 - Vehicle Health Module
**Vehicle Health Insights**
- Show overall vehicle health status
- Display battery level and driving range
- Show tire pressure state
- Display odometer information
- Give users a quick snapshot of vehicle condition

## Slide 12 - Document Management Module
**Document Tracking**
- Store vehicle-related document details
- Record insurance, registration, and other important records
- Save document expiry dates
- Highlight expired documents visually
- Act as a digital glovebox for the user

## Slide 13 - Emergency SOS Module
**SOS Emergency Assistance**
- Save up to 5 emergency contacts
- Trigger SOS with a short countdown to avoid accidental use
- Send SMS alerts with GPS location
- Continue live location sharing during emergency mode
- Allow user to stop SOS when safe
- Maintain SOS history for reference

## Slide 14 - Eco Impact Module
**Eco Impact Tracking**
- Record vehicle efficiency improvement after servicing
- Calculate fuel saved after better servicing
- Estimate CO2 emissions reduced
- Convert savings into tree-equivalent impact
- Generate eco score and achievement badges
